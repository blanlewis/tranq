Your Perfect Retreat by Maple Beach.

Experience unparalleled tranquility at Tranquil Homestay, strategically located just 400 meters from the pristine shores of Malpe Beach in Karnataka. Immerse yourself in the serene ambiance and make lasting memories with our exceptional facilities and warm hospitality.

  • Comfortable rooms with optional AC and non-AC configurations.
  • Ample parking space for your convenience.
  • Enjoy hot water facilities and modern amenities, including Android TV.
  • Experience the joy of a family get-together in our ideal setting.
  • Indulge in BBQ delights with our fully-equipped kitchen.
  • Benefit from taxi facilitation for seamless travel.

Whether you're seeking a peaceful escape or a family gathering, our homestay is the perfect choice. We are conveniently located 6 kilometers from N.H.66, making us easily accessible from Udupi Railway Station (11 km) and Mangalore Airport (65 km).
